Flex's Piracy - Our Opinion on Piracy

Flex Studios is in hot water after it was revealed that they allegedly told users to visit websites offering pirated movies. Many believe that guiding people to pirated content is just as bad as stealing it. While the CEO of Flex Studios hasn't commented, partner Hanan Nadeem responded casually, saying, “And,” which has upset many users who are concerned about the company's values. On the other hand, RA10 Studios strongly opposes piracy. They have made it clear that SkamHub v3 will not have any pirated content or suggestions for finding such materials. RA10 Studios believes in supporting creators and keeping content legal and ethical. As this situation unfolds, it highlights the importance of choosing platforms that respect content creators and promote good practices.

Written on: 20 September, 2024 by RA10 Studios

Why does SkamHub v3 have no AI?

After careful consideration, we have made the decision to delay the launch of SkamHub's AI integration

At SkamHub, we prioritise quality and user experience above all else. Rushing to release an AI feature while it's still in beta could lead to unintended issues or performance problems. Rather than compromising on reliability, we’ve chosen to hold off on integrating AI until we are 100% confident that it meets our high standards.

While SkamHub v3 may not yet feature AI, we’re proud to introduce SkamHub Assistant, a personal assistant available to all users for free. Unlike AI, SkamHub Assistant isn’t powered by machine learning but instead serves as a reliable and streamlined tool to help you navigate the platform and improve your overall experience.

We are committed to delivering the best version of SkamHub, and that includes a flawless AI system when it’s ready. Pushing back the release allows us to avoid potential issues that could arise from launching a beta product. We believe in offering perfection, not just "good enough."

We appreciate your patience and support as we work to make SkamHub v3 a platform you can trust, knowing that when AI arrives, it will be the best it can be. Thank you for being part of the SkamHub community!

Written on: 24 September, 2024 by SkamHub Team
